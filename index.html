About Me

My name is Jordan Rigsby. After graduating from the University of Central Florida in 2013 with a degree in English Creative Writing and plans to join the esteemed world of publishing, my plans were changed after taking a position with a publishing startup out of London where I found that publishing was not to my taste afterall. Since then I have continued my career in the field of digital marketing, where I was introduced to coding and development.
