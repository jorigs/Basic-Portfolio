About Me

My name is Jordan Rigsby. After graduating from the University of Central Florida in 2013 with a degree in English Creative Writing and plans to join the esteemed world of publishing, my plans were changed after taking a position with a publishing startup out of London. Since then I have continued my career in the field of digital marketing, where I was introduced to coding and development.

Space, the final frontier. These are the voyages of the Starship Enterprise. Its five-year mission: to explore strange new worlds, to seek out new life and new civilizations, to boldly go where no man has gone before.

As I stand out here in the wonders of the unknown at Hadley, I sort of realize there’s a fundamental truth to our nature, Man must explore . . . and this is exploration at its greatest.

To be the first to enter the cosmos, to engage, single-handed, in an unprecedented duel with nature—could one dream of anything more?
